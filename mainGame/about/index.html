About Typing Game

Typing Game is a modern, interactive web application designed to help you improve your typing speed and accuracy in a fun and competitive way.

Features:

  • Create an account and log in securely through an authentication system.
  • Compete for the top spot on the Leaderboard by achieving the highest scores.
  • Enjoy multiple play modes:
    • Race Against the Words: Type the words before they fade out from the screen.
    • Time Attack: Type as many words as you can within a set time limit.
  • Track your progress and challenge your friends!

Whether you're looking to sharpen your typing skills or compete with others, Typing Game offers a beautiful, user-friendly experience for everyone.
